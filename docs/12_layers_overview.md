# The 12 Layers of a SaaS Application

This document provides a high-level overview of the 12 functional layers of a modern SaaS application, from core data management to analytics and compliance.

## The 12 Layers

1. **Master Data & Core**: Organizations/tenants, users, contacts, products.
2. **Market & Leads**: Campaigns, leads, scoring, interactions.
3. **Opportunities**: Pipeline, stage history, opportunity products, forecasts.
4. **Quotes & RFQs**: RFQs, quotes, line items, approvals, alternatives.
5. **Proposals & Negotiation**: Proposals, sections, negotiation points, deal terms.
6. **Contracts & Orders**: Contracts, purchase orders, change orders, amendments.
7. **Delivery & Projects**: Projects, milestones, tasks, resources, deliverables.
8. **Billing & Revenue**: Invoices, payments, revenue recognition, AR aging.
9. **Success & Support**: Cases, SLAs, health scores, feedback, knowledge base.
10. **Renewal & Upsell**: Renewal opportunities, expansions, churn-prevention, renewal history.
11. **Analytics & Reporting**: Pipeline metrics, revenue metrics, team performance, custom reports.
12. **Audit & System Logs**: Audit logs, user activity, system events, data quality, compliance trail.
